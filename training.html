Project Based English Language Training

Improve your English proficiency by developing a project to showcase your expertise! Enhance team building! Develop communication skills!

Tell us your experience, knowledge and skill and we will help you develop a project to showcase them. We will explore the value and potential benefits of your particular skill or expertise and build a team for you. Your team can bring a project to life by documenting the process.

This is an example of what can be achieved! (Sustainable Energy Practice on Small Scale Farms Cagayan Report.pdf)



The project “Sustainable Energy Practice on a Small Farm Cagayan Report” was created by Ric B. Balinoyos, Jerald L. Orpilla, Alwin Padua and Rolando Batallones who documented the sustainable practice of anaerobic digestion to make use of pig manure in order to produce methane gas for household use and fertilizer.

We encourage you to explore the wealth of knowledge and experience you have in agriculture to showcase your achievements and build a better future.
